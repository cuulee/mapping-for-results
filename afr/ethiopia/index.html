ETHIOPIA

Since the end of a devastating civil war in 1991, the World Bank has supported the Government of Ethiopia through 60 operations and more than US$7.6 billion. Important results include a near two-fold increase in the number of children in primary school between 2001 and 2008, a reduction in child mortality to 109 in 2010 from 204 in 1990, increased rural access to safe water to almost 62 percent in 2009 from only 19 percent in 1990, all the while building local government capacity for service delivery and increased accountability.

Active financed activities as of April 12, 2011.
