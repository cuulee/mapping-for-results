NEPAL

Nepal is a country at a crossroads, emerging from a decade-long conflict that formally ended in November 2006 and now transitioning from conflict to peace and from a monarchy to a republic. Growth rebounded to 4.7 percent in fiscal year 2008 but the next few years are critical if Nepal is to build on this success, managing its transition while confronting long-standing development challenges at a time of global economic downturn.
