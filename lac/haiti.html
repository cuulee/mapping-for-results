Following the January 12, 2010 earthquake in Haiti, the World Bank Group restructured its Haiti portfolio by reallocating resources and modifying activities toward urgent post-earthquake priorities. The World Bank Group pledged US$479 million for the first two years and has cancelled Haiti's remaining US$39 million debt to IDA.

The following interactive map shows the World Bank's active project portfolio in Haiti as of January 2011 (including IDA and various grant-financed projects).
